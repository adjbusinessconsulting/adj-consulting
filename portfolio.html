Unlocking Customer Value
Through RFM Segmentation

Bisnis ritel online sering gagal membedakan pelanggan loyal dari yang hampir tidak aktif. Proyek ini menggunakan RFM Analysis pada 123MB data transaksi nyata untuk mengidentifikasi segmen pelanggan bernilai tinggi — membantu bisnis mengalokasikan anggaran marketing lebih efisien.

Masalah yang Dipecahkan

Perusahaan ritel online menghabiskan anggaran promosi secara merata ke semua pelanggan tanpa memahami siapa yang benar-benar berharga. Pelanggan setia tidak mendapat perhatian khusus, sementara pelanggan tidak aktif terus dibiayai kampanye yang sia-sia.

Yang Kami Lakukan

EDA mendalam pada data UCI Online Retail, lalu menghitung skor RFM per pelanggan. Setiap pelanggan diberi skor 1-5 untuk Recency, Frequency, dan Monetary — kemudian dikelompokkan menjadi 4 segmen strategis dan divisualisasikan dalam Power BI dashboard interaktif.

So, What Should the Business Do?
// recommendation · step_by_step_action_plan · slides 34-36
01
Reactivate Lapsing / Inactive Customers
Send a time-limited reactivation message (email or push notification) offering a simple incentive such as free shipping or a small discount (10%).
// why it works → Urgency lowers the barrier to return and encourages customers to re-engage with minimal marketing cost.
02
Build Loyalty (Primary Focus)
Introduce a basic loyalty mechanism — member card or points system — where customers earn points after each purchase. Points can be collected and exchanged for gifts.
// why it works → Creates a reason to return, shifting behavior from one-time purchases to repeat buying. Small rewards encourage habit formation.
03
Increase Spending (Primary Focus)
Offer bundle deals (e.g., phone case + charger at a discounted price) or add-on discounts at checkout. Send email whenever promotions or discounts are created.
// why it works → Bundling increases basket size without requiring additional customer acquisition, directly improving monetary value while keeping pricing attractive.

Performance & Profitability

Dari 485,875 item inventaris, hanya 36.47% yang terjual. Proyek ini menganalisis Looker E-commerce dataset untuk mengungkap kategori terlaris, brand paling menguntungkan, produk slow-moving, dan performa antar distribution center.

Masalah yang Dipecahkan

Lebih dari 63% modal terikat di stok yang tidak bergerak. Bisnis perlu memahami produk mana yang menghasilkan profit, mana yang terlalu lama di gudang, dan distribution center mana yang paling efisien.

Yang Kami Lakukan

// the_so_what_moment
So, What Should the Business Do?
// recommendations · notebook section 7 & 8
01
Increase stock allocation for high-revenue and high-profit categories
Certain categories contribute the highest revenue. Focus inventory investment here — ensuring top-performing products are never out of stock.
02
Review slow-moving products — discount, bundle, or reduce reorder volume
Some products take significantly longer to sell. Consider discounting, bundling, or stopping reorders — capital tied up in slow stock could be redeployed to products that actually sell.
03
Prioritize brands with consistently high revenue and healthy profit margins
Brand-level performance differs substantially. Focus marketing and shelf space on brands that consistently deliver strong margins.
04
Investigate distribution centers with slower average inventory turnover
DCs may have different turnover speeds, signaling operational inefficiencies or regional demand variation. Slower DCs need operational review.
05
Use monthly trend patterns to improve procurement and inventory planning
Monthly sales trends reveal fluctuations that support stock planning and seasonal forecasting. Don't over-stock during slow months or under-stock during peaks.

Segmentasi manual bersifat subjektif. Proyek ini menggunakan K-Means Clustering untuk menemukan pengelompokan pelanggan yang lebih natural dan sepenuhnya berbasis data.

Delivery Time with ML

Platform delivery sering memberikan estimasi waktu yang tidak akurat, menyebabkan pelanggan kecewa. Proyek ini membangun model ML untuk memprediksi waktu pengiriman secara akurat — mempertimbangkan jarak, cuaca, traffic, dan pengalaman kurir.

Masalah yang Dipecahkan

Jika ETA tidak akurat, pelanggan kecewa, restoran dapat keluhan, dan tim operasional kesulitan mengalokasikan kurir. Model prediksi ML memungkinkan platform memberikan ETA yang lebih realistis.

So, What Should the Business Do?
// business_recommendations · notebook section 7.2
01
Use predictive ETA models in the app
Show customers more realistic delivery estimates. The model predicts with R²=0.82 and MAE of only ±6 minutes — far more accurate than generic "30-60 minute" estimates.
// why it works → Realistic ETAs reduce customer disappointment and build platform trust.
02
Allocate couriers more strategically
Assign experienced couriers to difficult routes or busy periods. Distance and courier experience are both significant factors — smarter assignment reduces delays.
// why it works → Matching the right courier to the right route improves performance.
03
Adjust ETA during peak hours
Incorporate rush hour and traffic conditions into operational planning. Traffic level and time of day both impact delivery time — ETAs should automatically adjust.
// why it works → Proactive adjustments reduce customer complaints before they happen.
04
Monitor preparation time together with distance
Delivery delay is not only caused by courier travel but also by kitchen readiness. Distance × Prep_Time was one of the strongest predictors.
// why it works → Fixing kitchen bottlenecks can reduce total delivery time just as much as optimizing courier routes.
05
Use the model as a decision-support tool
The model can help operations teams anticipate delays and improve service quality — informing when to proactively communicate with customers about potential delays.
// why it works → Data-driven operations teams respond faster and more accurately than those relying on intuition alone.
