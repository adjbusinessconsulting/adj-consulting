How It Works

Selling your durian
is simple.

1
Contact Us

WhatsApp or call us to let us know your harvest size, variety, and location. We'll respond within a few hours.

2
We Assess

We assess the quality and grade of your durian fairly and transparently based on our standard criteria.

3
Agree on Price

We discuss and agree on a fair price together. No pressure, no hidden deductions. You're always in control.

4
Get Paid

Payment is made immediately — cash on the spot or same-day bank transfer. No waiting, no delays.
